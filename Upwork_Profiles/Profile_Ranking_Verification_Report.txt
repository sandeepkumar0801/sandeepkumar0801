================================================================================
                    HOW TO TEST YOUR PROFILES YOURSELF
================================================================================

METHOD 1: SEARCH YOUR OWN PROFILE (After Publishing)

1. Publish your profile on Upwork
2. Use a different account or ask a friend to search
3. Search for your primary keywords:
   - ".NET Developer"
   - "Full Stack Developer"
   - "React Developer"
   - "AI Engineer"
4. See where your profile appears in results
5. Note: You may not see your own profile in search results

METHOD 2: TRACK PROFILE VIEWS

1. After publishing, monitor your profile views
2. Track which keywords bring the most views
3. Compare views before/after optimization
4. Note: Profile views don't guarantee ranking, but indicate visibility

METHOD 3: MONITOR INVITATIONS

1. Track how many invitations you receive
2. Note which job postings you're invited to
3. See if invitations match your keywords
4. Note: Invitations depend on JSS and other factors too

METHOD 4: USE UPWORK'S PROFILE STRENGTH INDICATOR

1. Check your profile strength meter on Upwork
2. Aim for "Strong" or "Complete" profile
3. This is Upwork's own assessment of your profile

METHOD 5: COMPARE WITH TOP-RANKED PROFILES

1. Search for top freelancers in your niche
2. Analyze their profile structure
3. Compare keyword placement
4. See if your profile matches their optimization level
